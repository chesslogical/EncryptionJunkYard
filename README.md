Search git for rust encryption apps and you will find a bloated pile of apps, all with numerous files. I thought that is just plain silly. Here are lots of tiny apps. Take the main.rs from any app here, feed it to ai (chatgpt or grok or whatever) and BAM! Easy to change, audit, update, whatever. This junk yard has it all! OTP, aes variants, xchacha20 variants, serpent, threefish1024, and more.
